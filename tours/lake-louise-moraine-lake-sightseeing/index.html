About This Experience

Lake Louise and Moraine Lake are two of the most famous natural landmarks in North America ‚Äî and for good reason. The colors of these glacier-fed lakes have to be seen to be believed, and seeing them with a guide who can share the geology, the ecology, and the best-kept viewpoint secrets makes all the difference.

Itinerary

8:30 AM
Hotel Pickup
Convenient morning pickup from your Banff or Canmore hotel. Settle in for the scenic drive through the Bow Valley.
9:30 AM
Lake Louise
Arrive at Lake Louise for guided exploration. Walk along the shoreline, learn about Victoria Glacier, and capture photos with the Chateau in the background.
11:15 AM
Moraine Lake
Drive to Moraine Lake and hike the short Rockpile Trail for the iconic Valley of the Ten Peaks viewpoint. Explore the lakeside at your own pace.

Scenic Return
Return to your hotel with an optional stop for additional photos along the Bow Valley Parkway.
1:30 PM
Hotel Drop-off
Back at your hotel with the whole afternoon free. Your guide will recommend restaurants and activities for the rest of your day.

Questions About This Tour

The Lake Louise shoreline is paved and accessible. However, the Rockpile Trail at Moraine Lake involves uneven terrain and stairs. Please contact us to discuss your specific needs and we'll work to accommodate you.
If you'd like to add canoeing, check out our "Lake Louise & Moraine Lake Tour with Canoe" option. It includes everything in this tour plus a guided canoe experience on Two Jack Lake.
This tour runs rain or shine ‚Äî the mountains look incredible in all weather. We only cancel for unsafe road conditions. If we cancel, you receive a full refund or free rescheduling.
