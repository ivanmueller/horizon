About This Experience

There's a reason Lake Louise is one of the most photographed places in the world ‚Äî and there's a reason most photos don't do it justice. The turquoise water, the glacier backdrop, the scale of it all ‚Äî it has to be experienced firsthand.

This early morning canoe experience gets you on the water before the day-trippers arrive.
