Built for the hotel concierge

Three things hotels actually need from an affiliate program

Most tour-referral kits are designed for operators. Horizon Connect is designed for the front desk first — every feature exists to make the concierge's job easier and to make the payout obviously fair.

QR codes for every touchpoint

Issue a unique short URL + QR for the lobby kiosk, each concierge, every in-room card, and every campaign. Scans, sessions, and bookings are stitched into a multi-touch funnel so credit lands on the touchpoint that actually drove the guest.

Multi-touch attribution, not last-click guessing

Every touchpoint a guest hits — kiosk, in-room QR, staff link, paid placement — is recorded with a timestamp and a code. Connect's attribution policies (first-touch, last-touch, time-decayed) give you a defensible answer when a payout is disputed.

Direct payouts on a fixed schedule

Hotels are paid net-30 to their own Stripe account. No invoicing, no chasing operators for a cheque. Connect collects a flat percent of the transaction for routing — visible on every line item so there's nothing to argue about.

How it works

From handshake to first payout in under a week

Connect is a thin layer between hotels and the operators they already work with. No new POS, no embedded booking engine to fight your existing reservation system.

1
Connect your operator partners

Pick the operators in your area you want to refer. Connect handles the contracts and commission rates — most hotels are routing through 4–6 operators in their first week.

2
Place QR codes and short URLs

We print and ship the lobby card, the in-room QR, and the per-concierge link cards. Every scan or click is captured before the guest even hits the operator's site.

3
Get paid net-30, automatically

When the guest's booking confirms, the commission is logged, attribution is sealed, and payout queues for net-30. Watch it in your dashboard or wait for the line item in your bank.
