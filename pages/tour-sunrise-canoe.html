Overview

Experience the magic of Lake Louise before the world wakes up. This guided sunrise canoe adventure puts you on the turquoise waters at golden hour, paddling beneath the Victoria Glacier with the Rockies reflected around you. It's the most iconic Canadian Rockies moment ‚Äî and you'll have it almost to yourself.

Our expert local guide shares the geology, ecology, and Indigenous history of this sacred landscape while ensuring you get the perfect photographs. Limited to 12 guests for an intimate, unhurried experience.

Itinerary

5:30 AM

Hotel Pickup

We collect you from your Banff or Canmore accommodation in our comfortable Ford Transit. Relax and enjoy a coffee as we drive through the Bow Valley with early morning mountain views.

6:15 AM

Arrive at Lake Louise

Arrive at Lake Louise before the crowds. Your guide provides a brief paddling orientation and safety talk. Everyone gets fitted with life jackets and paddles.

6:30 AM

Sunrise Paddle

Launch onto the turquoise waters as sunrise light hits the Victoria Glacier. Paddle the length of the lake, pausing for photos and learning about the geology and ecology from your guide. This is the moment everyone remembers.

7:30 AM

Lakeside Exploration

Return to shore and explore the iconic lakeshore on foot. Visit the viewpoint that graces a million postcards, hear the stories of the Chateau, and enjoy the serenity before the day-trippers arrive.

8:30 AM

Return Journey

Depart Lake Louise feeling like you've unlocked a secret. Drive back through the Bow Valley with a newfound appreciation for these mountains. Return to your hotel by approximately 9:00 AM ‚Äî the whole day still ahead of you.
