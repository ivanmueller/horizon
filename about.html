Our Story

From a Ford Transit and a Dream

Wild Atlas Tours was born from a simple frustration: the big tour buses were hauling 50+ people to the same viewpoints, spending five minutes, and driving on. The most beautiful places on earth were being treated like a drive-through.

We believed there was a better way ‚Äî small groups, expert guides who actually live here, strategic timing that avoids the crowds, and enough space to actually breathe in the mountain air. So we got our commercial license, insured up, and started running the tours we wished existed.

Today, we operate from Banff and Canmore with a focus on Lake Louise, Moraine Lake, and the hidden gems most tourists never find. Every guest leaves as a friend. That's the standard.
