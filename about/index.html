Why we built the canoe experience

When we started Horizon, we noticed something: everyone wanted to canoe in Banff, but the options were limited to rental docks with hour-long queues and 30-minute time slots. That's not an experience ‚Äî that's a transaction.

So we did something different. We bring our own canoes to Two Jack Lake, where there are no rental shops and no crowds. Just you, the water, and Mount Rundle. It's the kind of experience we wish someone had offered us, and now it's something only our guests can do.
